# Product Overview

## Epsionyx Company - Student Success Platform

A comprehensive educational technology platform that transforms academic content into industry-ready skills. The platform helps students bridge the gap between university theory and professional practice by:

- **Document Processing**: Upload academic PDFs and extract key learning topics
- **Industry Mapping**: Generate real-world industry tasks and practical exercises for each topic
- **Portfolio Development**: Create hands-on projects that demonstrate industry application
- **Interview Preparation**: Provide comprehensive interview questions and scenarios
- **Career Readiness**: Bridge academic knowledge with professional requirements

## Core Value Proposition

Transform university education into practical, industry-relevant skills that prepare students for successful employment transitions.

## Target Users

- University students seeking practical application of academic concepts
- Recent graduates preparing for job interviews
- Educational institutions wanting to enhance curriculum relevance
- Career counselors and academic advisors

## Key Features

- AI-powered document analysis and topic extraction
- Industry-specific task generation
- Comprehensive interview question banks
- Real-world case study scenarios
- Progress tracking and learning analytics
